Background

In class I learned that microinteractions engage users and brings delight in small moments. To apply this knowledge, I envisioned and developed my own microinteractions in the span of 10 weeks. I chose Pinterest as an interesting website/app to redesign for because of the potential to pull in the branding into the microninteraction.

Problem

Throughout the evolution of my microinteraction, I had to consider what could bring delight without loosing the existing functionality. I also had to consider the feasability within the timeline I was given with 1-2 weeks for each level of microinteraction.
